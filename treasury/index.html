Before he is ready to set sail though, he wants to be a little more prepared. He knows of a room that is used to store goods that have been "reclaimed" from various sailing groups.

He asks that you and your friends make into the room and find anything that might be useful in future battles.

Below the shelves is a series of the 6 colored gems - white, red, yellow, green, blue and orange. Upon a closer investigation, it looks like the gems under the shelves can be pushed in slightly, but not removed.
